Sección de Gramática

Comparative Adjectives

Comparativos de igualdad: "as... as..."

Ejemplo: She is as tall as her brother.

Comparativos de superioridad e inferioridad: "more | less than"

Ejemplo: This book is more interesting than that one.

Calificadores: a lot, a bit, a little, enough, really, fairly, too

Ejemplo: He is a bit tired. / This movie is really good.

Comparative Adverbs

Uso de adverbios comparativos para describir acciones.

Ejemplo: She runs faster than him.
